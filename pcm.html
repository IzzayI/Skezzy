Academic year 2020/2021 - Semester 3

Personal goals

Strength and Weakness



Everybody has strengths and weaknesses.
I aspire to highlight strengths and want to utilize them to achieve the common goal.
I aspire to turn weaknesses into strengths.

This goal is related to the ABI's because:
I am working on my professional attitude through identifying my own strengths and using them to the teams benefits.
Additinoally, goal helps me accomplish my responsibility and accountability for my own work load.


How am I planning on achieving this goal:
  1. Defining my own strengths and weaknesses through the belbin role workshop
  2. Discuss the strengths and weaknesses with every group member
  3. Divide tasks and responsibility accordingly

My Strengths

As a result of the Belbin Role Workshop, I am someone who enjoys challenges where I can use my mind, whether I have to come up with new ideas or weigh up options and decide on the best way forward.
My second strength was my interest to help other people to maintain balanced group dynamic

Applied to a group

I saw my opportunity to jump in and help the group as soon they appeared to get stuck and went quiet in a meeting. Partially, I found myself encouraged by the result of the Belbin roles. On the other hand, I had difficulties sticking to my plan of having an open disscussion in the beginning of group work, because most member did not see it as necessary, or because of time issues.
